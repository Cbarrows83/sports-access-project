Access Sports Initiative

Making Golf & Snow Sports More Accessible

This project focuses on expanding access to golf and snow sports for students by addressing cost, transportation, and opportunity barriers.

Why It Matters

Some sports stay out of reach for too many students

Golf, skiing, and snowboarding can build confidence, discipline, and lifelong interests, but many students never get the chance to participate because of the cost of equipment, travel, and entry fees.

This website explores those barriers and highlights practical solutions that could make these sports more available at the high school level.
