Opening the Slopes

The Problem

Skiing and snowboarding can be difficult for students to access because of the cost of lift tickets, equipment rentals, and transportation.

Barriers

  • Expensive lift tickets
  • Equipment rentals
  • Transportation to mountains
  • Lessons for beginners

Solutions

Schools could organize group ski trips, partner with resorts for discounted lift tickets, and provide rental gear for students.
