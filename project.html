About the Project

1. Project Purpose

This project explores how certain sports remain inaccessible to many students due to financial barriers and lack of opportunities.

2. Research

Research was conducted on the costs associated with golf and snow sports, as well as potential programs schools could implement.

3. Proposed Solutions

The project proposes equipment sharing, partnerships with local facilities, and school-organized programs to reduce barriers.

4. Reflection

This project highlights how access to sports can influence student experiences and opportunities.
