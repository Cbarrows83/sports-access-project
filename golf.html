Making Golf Accessible

The Problem

Golf is often seen as an expensive sport because equipment, course fees, and lessons can cost a lot. This prevents many students from even trying the sport.

Barriers Students Face

  • Expensive clubs
  • Green fees
  • Lack of transportation
  • Limited beginner programs

Solutions

Schools could create equipment sharing programs, partner with local golf courses, and offer beginner clinics for students.
