Making Golf Accessible

The Problem

Golf is often seen as expensive due to equipment, course fees, and lessons, preventing many students from participating.

Barriers

  • Cost of clubs
  • Course fees
  • Transportation
  • Lack of beginner programs

Solutions

Schools could create equipment sharing programs, partner with local courses, and offer beginner clinics.
